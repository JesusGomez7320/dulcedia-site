From Hollywood Sets to Wedding Vows

There’s an immense joy in capturing love — not just as a witness, but as a storyteller. For me, wedding videography isn’t about documentation; it’s about crafting a visual narrative that reflects the depth, beauty, and emotion of one of life’s most meaningful chapters.

Before returning to my hometown of Orange County, I spent 8 years immersed in the film industry after graduating from film school in Los Angeles. That chapter of my life taught me how to shape mood with light, movement, and sound — and how to turn fleeting moments into timeless scenes.

Today, I bring that cinematic sensibility to every wedding I film across Southern California. I work closely with each couple, getting to know your vision, your dynamic, and the atmosphere you want to create. Whether it’s an elegant celebration beneath string lights or a spontaneous elopement on the coast, my approach is intentional, flexible, and deeply personal.

Every couple’s story is unique — and your film should be too. My goal is to create something that not only reflects your day, but makes you feel it all over again, every time you watch.

A Love Story in Motion: The Experience

Your wedding day is one of the most meaningful stories you’ll ever live — and I’m here to help you relive it, beautifully.

From our first conversation to the final delivery, I work closely with you to bring your vision to life. I focus on the details, capture the in-between moments, and craft a film that feels like you.

My goal is simple: to go above and beyond so your wedding is remembered not just clearly — but emotionally.
